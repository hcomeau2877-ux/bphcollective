The Origin Story
Three kids from Montréal who learned to read a play before they could read a contract.

Hubert, Patrick and Tommy met on the ice more than ten years ago. Middle-class, no shortcuts, no inheritance — just the kind of trust you only build when you've been in the corner together. One went into law. One into engineering and the field. One into deals and the rooms where they get made. Different paths, same instinct: build something that lasts longer than the next contract.
